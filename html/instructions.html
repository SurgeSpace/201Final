How To Play
  • Goal
  • The goal of the game is simple! Just pop all the bubbles before you run out of clicks!
  • Rules
  • Each bubble has a certain number of clicks before it pops.
  • When a bubble pops all the bubbles directly touching it get +1.
  • When all the bubbles are popped that level is over and you win!
  • If you run out of clicks before all the bubbles are popped you lose and have to play the level again.
  • Scoring
  • You get points for each bubble you pop!
  • When the level is over you get points for clicks remaining.
  • If you get the high score you win One MILLION Dollars!!!
